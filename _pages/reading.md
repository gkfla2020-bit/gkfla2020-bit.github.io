## 2026년 1월

### Week 4 (1/20 - 1/26)

**[The Kelly Criterion: Optimal Position Sizing](https://quantstart.com/articles/kelly-criterion/)**

**[Mean Reversion Strategies in Python](https://www.quantconnect.com/tutorials/strategy-library/mean-reversion)**  
평균회귀 전략 구현 튜토리얼. 볼린저밴드, Z-score 기반 진입/청산 로직.
