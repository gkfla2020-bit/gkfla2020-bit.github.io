### Market-Neutral Strategy Using Cryptocurrency Funding Rates

*FIND-A Financial Data Analysis Society, 2025*

Developed a market-neutral trading strategy exploiting funding rate differentials in cryptocurrency perpetual futures markets.

**Methodology**:
- Beta-matched long/short portfolio construction
- Liquidity filtering and pump detection algorithms
- Transaction cost and funding fee modeling

**Results**:
- 6-year backtest CAGR: 15.2%
- Sharpe Ratio: 1.24
- Maximum Drawdown: -23.4%

**Technical Implementation**: Python, Binance API, pandas, scikit-learn

### Quality Factor Long-Short Strategy in Cryptocurrency Markets

*FIND-A Financial Data Analysis Society, 2025*

Applied traditional quality factor investing principles to cryptocurrency markets through a systematic long-short approach.

**Strategy Design**:
- Long: BTC, ETH (high market cap, network effects)
- Short: Declining DeFi tokens with pump avoidance logic

**Risk Management**:
- Stop-loss: -15%
- Take-profit: +25%
- Maximum short loss cap: -30%

**Results** (2021-2024):
- Total Return: 42.8%
- CAGR: 9.4%
- Sharpe Ratio: 0.87
